About Travel Guard®

Part of Zurich Cover-More — delivering trusted travel insurance to Vietnam travellers for over two decades.

Travel Guard® is now part of Zurich Insurance Group

AIG's global personal travel insurance and assistance business, including Travel Guard®, is now part of Zurich Insurance Group under the Zurich Cover-More brand. This means you continue to receive the same trusted coverage and service, now backed by the global strength of one of the world's leading insurance providers.

The policy is underwritten by Zurich Insurance Company Ltd (Vietnam Branch), a licensed insurer authorised by the Monetary Authority of Vietnam (MAS).

Travel assistance and claim services are administered by Travel Guard Asia Pacific Pte Ltd (TGAP). Our policies are protected under the Policy Owners' Protection Scheme administered by the Vietnam Deposit Insurance Corporation (SDIC).
