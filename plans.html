Compare Travel Insurance for Vietnamese Travellers

Travel insurance shouldn't be complicated.

We offer three straightforward plans that let you choose the coverage you need. Whether you're looking for essential protection, balanced value, or comprehensive premium benefits, compare our plans to find the one that's right for your destination, activities and travel budget. All plans are tailored for travellers departing from Vietnam.

Basic Plan

Smart and Simple Travel Insurance for Quick Getaways

Perfect for spontaneous trips, regional travel, or budget-friendly escapes. The Basic Plan keeps things simple by covering the essential risks that can derail your holiday with clear benefits and no unnecessary extras. It's affordable travel protection that lets you travel light but not unprotected.

Why Vietnamese choose the Basic Plan

Key Travel Insurance Benefits

Up to S$100,000 in overseas medical coverage (under age 70) - essential even for short regional trips.

Emergency evacuation up to S$50,000, an important safety net when travelling somewhere unfamiliar.

Trip cancellation up to S$5,000 to protect pre-paid bookings when plans change unexpectedly.

Protection for lost baggage and travel documents, covering realistic everyday travel mishaps.

Affordable, no-frills coverage for travellers who want essential protection at a price that fits their budget.

Quick and hassle-free online claims for a smoother experience.

Enhanced Plan

Balanced Travel Insurance Coverage for Confident Travellers

Ideal for family holidays, long-haul trips or destinations with higher medical care costs. The Enhanced Plan offers comprehensive protection with stronger medical benefits, broader trip coverage and support that continues even after you return to Vietnam.

Why Vietnamese choose the Enhanced Plan

Key Travel Insurance Benefits

S$1,000,000 in overseas medical coverage (under age 70), suitable for long-haul destinations and countries with higher healthcare costs.

Unlimited emergency medical evacuation, providing full support in the event of a serious incident overseas.

Coverage for trip interruption, early return, and travel supplier financial default, protecting more complex or prepaid travel plans.

Coverage for medical treatment in Vietnam after returning home, a valuable safety net many basic plans don't offer.

Higher limits for baggage and travel delays, ideal for families or travellers carrying personal tech and shopping abroad.

Quick and hassle-free online claims for a smoother experience.

Supreme Plan

Premium Travel Insurance for No-Compromise Travel Coverage

Designed for travellers who appreciate higher-tier protection. The Supreme Plan delivers elevated benefits ideal for long-haul journeys to destinations such as the U.S., Europe and Japan or for cruise travel. With exceptional medical limits and enhanced financial safeguards, this plan is built for travellers who value a smoother, more secure experience wherever they go.

Why Vietnamese choose the Supreme Plan

Key Travel Insurance Benefits

S$2,000,000 in overseas medical coverage (under age 70), ideal for destinations with very high healthcare costs such as the U.S., Europe and Japan.

Unlimited emergency evacuation, and extensive support benefits for accompanying travellers and dependents.

Premium financial protection, including credit card indemnity and enhanced personal liability up to S$1,000,000.

Higher coverage for baggage, fraudulent credit card use, and travel documents designed for worry-free travel.

Highest trip cancellation and alteration limits, with Replacement of Traveller benefit for pre-departure needs.

Quick and hassle-free online claims for a smoother experience.
