Why Vietnam Travellers Trust Travel Guard® Travel Insurance

Travel Guard® travel insurance is designed for individuals departing from Vietnam. With high medical limits, reliable protection for travel disruptions, support when you need it most and fast digital claim filing for peace of mind. Our plans offer practical benefits that matter on real trips; from regional getaways to long-haul travel.

What makes Travel Guard® different?

  • High medical coverage with plan options up to S$2,000,000,ideal for destinations like the U.S., Europe and Japan – giving you confidence even in the most expensive healthcare markets.
  • Unlimited emergency evacuation optionsfor serious incidents overseas – get the emergency support you need without worrying about limits.
  • Trip cancellation and travel supplier financial default protectionon Enhanced and Supreme Plans – protects you when the unforeseen happens, leading you to cancel your trip before traveling.
  • Medical treatment after returning to Vietnam,available with Enhanced and Supreme Plans – protection that follows you home.
  • Optional cover for cruises and sports equipmentfor specialised travel needs.
  • Fast, hassle-free online claims for Vietnam travellers- submit and track digitally, with simple step-by-step support.
