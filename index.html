Why Vietnam Travellers Trust Travel Guard® Travel Insurance

Travel Guard® travel insurance is designed for individuals departing from Vietnam. With high medical limits, reliable protection for travel disruptions, support when you need it most and claim filing for peace of mind. Our plans offer practical benefits that matter on real trips; from regional getaways to long-haul travel.

What makes Travel Guard® different?

  • High medical coverage with plan options up to VND 3,000,000,000, ideal for destinations like the U.S., Europe and Japan – giving you confidence even in the most expensive healthcare markets.
  • Emergency evacuation in the event of a serious incident abroad - get the urgent assistance in Vietnamese and many other languages, completely worry-free.
  • Trip cancellation and postponement - protects you when the unforeseen happens, leading you to cancel your trip before traveling.
  • Medical treatment after returning to Vietnam - protection that follows you home.
  • File a claim quickly and easily, with a fast and professional payout process.

Travel Insurance FAQs

In December 2024, Zurich Insurance Group acquired AIG's global personal travel insurance and assistance businesses, including Travel Guard. This acquisition strengthens Zurich's travel insurance capabilities and expands its global footprint.
Zurich Cover-More is the combined entity formed by bringing together Travel Guard with Zurich's existing travel insurance businesses, Cover-More Group. It will operate globally under this umbrella.
Existing policyholders will continue to be covered and serviced in accordance with their current policy terms and conditions.

For policies issued prior to May 31, 2026, you may still contact AIG for assistance.

For policies issued on or after June 1, 2026, you will be assisted by the Travel Guard Vietnam team.
